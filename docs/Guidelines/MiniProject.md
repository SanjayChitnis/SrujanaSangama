# 6th Semester B.Tech CSE Mini Project (3 Credits)  
## AI-Integrated Product-Oriented Guidelines

---

## Purpose
- Build **industry-relevant, AI-enabled, product-focused mini projects**
- Prepare students for **final year product pipelines, startups, and IP creation**
- Ensure **deep learning, continuous progress, and real-world impact**

---

## Project Selection
- Prefer:
  - Extension of **existing projects** (same team or previous cohorts)
  - **Industry-mentored or sponsored** projects
  - **Government/defense/ISRO/Smart India Hackathon** problem statements
  - Recognized **private problem portals**
- Encourage **multidisciplinary problem domains**
- Prioritize projects with **continuity into final year capstone**

---

## Team Composition
- Encourage **multi-cohort teams** (junior + senior continuity)
- Include **students from multiple streams** (CSE, ECE, design, management etc.)
- Promote **role clarity** and distributed responsibility across members

---

## Product Orientation
- Every project must have a **product perspective**
  - Identify **target users and market need**
  - Conduct **continuous user interaction and feedback loops**
  - Demonstrate **value creation and usability**
- Track metrics like **potential users, adoption feasibility, or monetization pathway**

---

## Development Approach (AI-Augmented)
- Follow **Spec-Driven Development (SDD)**
- Use **AI coding assistants and agents** for productivity
- Emphasize:
  - Requirements specification
  - Version control discipline
  - Testing and deployment
- Students must demonstrate **understanding beyond AI-generated output**

---

## Learning Process & Rubrics
- Evaluation to prioritize:
  - **Process adherence**
  - **Learning depth**
  - **Problem complexity handled**
  - **Team contribution and ownership**
- Include **viva, demos, and reflective learning documentation**

---

## Collaboration & Open Ecosystem
- Encourage:
  - **External mentors**
  - **Industry collaborators**
  - **Open-source contributions**
- Exposure to **real development communities and workflows**

---

## Individual Accountability
- Ensure **fair contribution across team members**
- Mechanisms:
  - Individual task ownership logs
  - Peer evaluation
  - Code contribution analysis

---

## Continuous Progress Tracking
- Enforce **daily/regular meaningful Git commits**
- Avoid last-minute bulk submissions
- Maintain:
  - Sprint plans
  - Weekly reviews
  - Milestone tracking dashboards

---

## Experiential Learning Model
- Apply **Kolb’s Experiential Learning Cycle**
  - Concrete experience
  - Reflective observation
  - Abstract conceptualization
  - Active experimentation
- Integrate **design thinking and agile practices**

---

## Engagement Formats
- Mid-semester **hackathons / learnathons**
  - Enable **course correction and pivoting**
  - Provide **expert jury mentoring**
- Final **Project Exhibition / Demo Day**
  - Invite **industry, faculty, investors, and users**

---

## Academic Integrity
- **Strict prohibition** of:
  - Buying ready-made projects
  - Plagiarism or code copying
- Violations to result in **strong academic penalties**

---

## Mentorship & Support
- Ensure:
  - **Dedicated faculty mentors**
  - Access to **AI tools and platforms**
  - Domain and product mentors where needed
- Structured **review checkpoints and mentoring clinics**

---

## Additional International Best Practices to Integrate
- Agile/Scrum sprint cycles with stand-ups
- Design reviews and architecture reviews
- Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD)
- Product pitch decks and investor-style demos
- Documentation as per open-source standards
- Portfolio-ready GitHub repositories

---

## Action Items
1. Define **approved project themes and sources**
2. Create **AI-enabled spec-driven development SOP**
3. Develop **rubrics including process, contribution, and learning**
4. Set up **GitHub tracking and contribution analytics**
5. Schedule **mid-sem hackathons and final exhibition**
6. Identify and onboard **industry/external mentors**
7. Establish **academic integrity enforcement policy**
8. Create **faculty mentoring and FDP plan**
9. Define **student training modules on AI tools and product thinking**
10. Build **pipeline mapping from mini project → final year product**
